The delicious bread

Roti is a delicious flat bread to go with any meal such as Pakistani or indian curry's and is quick and simple to do

  • water

RECIPE

you guys ready for the recipe and just remember you don't have fireproof hands(even though some mums do)and also remember its not going to come out good the first time

    2. ok so you get a amount of flour you think can fill you and slowly add small amounts of water,it should clump together and make a fill dough

  2. Roll it out

    3. grab a large handful of dough and roll it out,before that remember to dust the table with flour and roll it a thin size(1cm with)

    4. put it onto a heated pan (very hot pan)and let both sides turn white before you but both sides over fire(unless your a asian mum use metal clamps to do so
